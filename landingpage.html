About This Project

JobFinder.AI was born from the idea that resume creation shouldn't be stressful or time-consuming. As a web development student, I wanted to combine design, automation, and AI into something truly helpful.

This is a portfolio project developed at HolbertonSchool Rodez and built with Node.js, React, Tailwind CSS, and OpenAI. It's designed to demonstrate practical full-stack and AI integration.
